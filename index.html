About

This website is my workspace to practise and try out AWS components as part of my personal project. Initially, when I had started, I was not much confident on serverless approach to creating my portfolio website. So I had gone old-school and started from scratch - creating VPC, subnets and spinning EC2 instances. Also, I did install wordpress on those servers and created the website. The running website used to cost me $30-$35 AUD every month. After couple of months, I realised that was just a waste of my money when I can do the same thing going serverless.

This is just that website of me trying to go serverless and will see how much cost efective this turns out.
